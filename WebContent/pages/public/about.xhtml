Outreach & Engagement

Who We Are

We help learners around the region achieve more. We support students, teachers, entities, corporates and institutions through a lifetime of learning by providing them with world-class content & mentor in the most relevant, engaging and flexible formats.

Vision

To be the preeminent service provider in Egypt for lifelong learning products and career related services.

Mission

The Outreach and Engagement (O&E) is established to be the umbrella under which Life Long Learning services and STEM training for teachers and K12 students can be offered by Zewail City. The O&E will be part of the interface of Zewail City with the Egyptian Community. Through the enablement of the O&E, different training and services offerings from the academic programs and research institutes can be offered to the community.
